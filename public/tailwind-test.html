Usage Examples

How to use these colors:

Background: bg-zuppie-500, bg-zuppie-pink-400, bg-package-birthday-500
Text: text-zuppie-600, text-zuppie-pink-500, text-success-500
Border: border-zuppie-300, border-zuppie-pink-200
Custom: .gradient-bg, .gradient-text, .btn-primary

All colors are defined in tailwind.config.js and can be easily modified from there.
